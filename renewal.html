Automatic Renewal Terms

To ensure uninterrupted access to premium features, LoVault uses automatic renewal.

1. Renewal Timing

Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current billing period.

2. Cancellation Policy

You can turn off auto-renewal at any time. This must be done at least 24 hours before the end of the current period to avoid being charged for the next cycle.

3. How to Manage

Go to your iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions to manage or cancel your LoVault subscription.
