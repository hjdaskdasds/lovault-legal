This Membership Agreement ("Agreement") explains the premium membership plans ("Membership") available within the lovault App. By purchasing a Membership, you agree to the terms in this Agreement.

1. Membership Tiers and Benefits

lovault offers the following membership options (subject to change):

  • Weekly Plan: Unlimited AI reply generations, premium tone styles, no advertisements. Billed weekly.
  • Monthly Plan: Same premium benefits billed monthly at a discounted rate.
  • Annual Plan: Best value – all premium benefits billed annually with a free trial period (if offered).

Exact features and pricing are displayed in the App's paywall and are confirmed at time of purchase.

2. Free Trial

We may offer a free trial period for select plans. The trial duration will be clearly shown before you subscribe. Unless you cancel at least 24 hours before the trial ends, it will automatically convert to a paid subscription. No charges apply if cancelled in time.

3. Subscription Management and Cancellation

You can manage or cancel your Membership at any time through your Apple ID account settings. Instructions can be found here: Apple Support. Uninstalling the app does not automatically cancel your subscription.

4. Refund Policy

As the payment is processed by Apple, refunds must be requested directly from Apple. We do not process refunds ourselves. Apple's refund policy can be reviewed at Request a refund for apps or content that you bought from Apple.

5. Price Changes

We may change the price of a Membership from time to time. If a price affects an existing recurring subscription, we will notify you in advance. Your continued subscription after the notice period constitutes acceptance of the new price.
