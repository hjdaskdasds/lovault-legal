    <h1>Privacy Policy for lovault</h1>

    <p class="effective-date"><strong>Effective Date:</strong> May 1, 2026</p>


    <p>

        lovault ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy.

        This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("the App"), which helps users craft emotionally intelligent replies.

    </p>


    <h2>1. Information We Collect</h2>

    <p>We collect limited information to provide and improve the App:</p>

    <ul>

        <li><strong>User Content.</strong> When you input text to receive high‑EQ reply suggestions, that text is processed temporarily. We do <strong>not</strong> store your input text on our servers after the generation is complete. Processing is done on‑device or via ephemeral sessions with our AI service provider, depending on your settings.</li>

        <li><strong>Usage Data.</strong> We collect anonymous analytics (e.g., feature usage frequency, crash logs, interaction patterns) via Apple’s built‑in frameworks. This data does not identify you personally.</li>

        <li><strong>Subscription Information.</strong> When you purchase a subscription, Apple processes the payment. We receive only a non‑identifiable subscriber status token to unlock premium features. We do <strong>not</strong> collect your credit card details.</li>

        <li><strong>Support Requests.</strong> If you contact us, we may keep your email address and message content solely to resolve your issue.</li>

    </ul>


    <h2>2. How We Use Information</h2>

    <p>We use the collected information to:</p>

    <ul>

        <li>Generate, deliver, and improve the emotional intelligence reply suggestions.</li>

        <li>Respond to your comments, questions, and support requests.</li>

        <li>Send important notices (e.g., subscription changes) if permitted by you.</li>

        <li>Comply with legal obligations.</li>

    </ul>
